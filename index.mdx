## Underwriting Superpowers

Milo allows you to underwrite Commercial Real Estate in minutes, by automating the early, labor intensive stages:

> **Upload Documents for Extraction** - Rent rolls, T12s, operating statements in any format

> **Setup your Deal** - Configure your buy box criteria and tune any and all deal parameters

> **Analyze Instantly** - Jump into financial modeling with structured data, error validations, and advanced insights
